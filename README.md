# Excel Data Analytics Portfolio

## Project: Sales Performance Analysis (2023)

### Business Problem
A retail business wanted to understand how to increase revenue 
across its product categories, regions, sales channels, and 
sales team. This project analyses 1,000 sales transactions 
from 2023 to uncover what's working and where the opportunities lie.

### Questions Explored
1. Which products/categories are the best and worst sellers?
2. Which regions are over- and underperforming?
3. Which sales channel drives more revenue — Online or Retail?
4. Which sales rep is the top performer?

### Key Findings

**Total Revenue:** $5,019,265.23  
**Average Transaction Value:** $5,019.27  
**Total Transactions:** 1,000

**Products & Categories**
- Clothing is the highest revenue-generating category ($1,313,474.36), 
  though performance varies by region, highlighting opportunities 
  to optimize stock regionally.
- Food is the lowest-performing category ($1,201,773.54).

**Regional Performance**
- The North region leads with $1,369,612.51 in revenue — a key 
  market for targeted growth initiatives.
- The South region is the weakest at $1,154,250.86.

**Sales Channel**
- Online slightly outperforms Retail (51% vs 49%), suggesting 
  digital channels are gaining traction and worth investing in further.

**Sales Rep Performance**
- Bob is the top-performing sales rep at $1,080,990.63.
- Charlie is the lowest at $860,811.48 — a gap of ~$220,000 
  worth investigating.

### Recommendations
- Ensure top-revenue products (Clothing) are always in stock, 
  especially during peak periods.
- Launch targeted campaigns to boost Food category sales.
- Increase investment in the Online channel to widen its lead.
- Replicate Bob's sales approach across the wider team — 
  investigate what's driving his performance.
- Deploy targeted growth initiatives in the South region 
  to close the gap with the North.

### Tools Used
Microsoft Excel — data cleaning, XLOOKUP, VLOOKUP, IF statements, 
pivot tables, dashboard (6 charts)
